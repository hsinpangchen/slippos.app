Use Slip where the counter needs to stay simple.

Cafes and market stalls do not always need a large POS suite. They usually need the essentials to stay close: a clear menu, fast orders, printed receipts, cash totals, and an end-of-day view they can trust.

01 · CAFE POS

Cafe POS for neighborhood coffee shops

Many cafes start small: a counter, a short menu, regular customers, and a busy rush that should not require a complicated system. Slip keeps the sales flow focused on what a small cafe actually needs day to day.

  • Catalog items for drinks, add-ons, pastries, and simple categories
  • Fast counter orders with receipt printing for clearer handoff
  • Daily close for cash payments, refunds, and end-of-day review
02 · MARKET STALL POS

Market stall POS for pop-ups

Market selling is often urgent: the line moves quickly, space is limited, and the team needs a simple way to keep orders and printed records organized on site.

  • Short menu grid for best sellers and fast repeat orders
  • Printed receipts that make on-site handoff and record keeping easier
  • Simple cash totals before packing up at the end of the day
