Offline iPad POS

An offline POS app for iPad, built for small shops that cannot stop the line.

Slip POS is designed for small shops, cafes, and market stalls that need iPad checkout to keep working when the internet drops. It stays focused on the floor: offline checkout, receipt printing, and cash reconciliation.

What this page is really about

If you're searching for an offline iPad POS, you're usually not looking for a huge all-in-one retail suite. You're looking for a POS app that keeps taking orders, printing receipts, and recording sales even when the network is unreliable.

Slip POS is built for exactly that kind of floor reality on iPad and iOS.

Why offline matters for an iPad POS

  • Your line should not stop because the shop Wi-Fi drops.
  • A market stall or pop-up may not have stable internet at all.
  • At close, you still need records and cash reconciliation to make sense.

Best fit for

  • Small shops that want a simpler iPad POS.
  • Cafes and food counters that need quick receipt printing.
  • Market stalls and pop-ups with unreliable connectivity.
  • Operators who care more about floor reliability than cloud back-office depth.

Built around iPad and iOS floor use

Slip POS centers the iPad counter workflow: fast checkout, printer connection, and end-of-day cash review.

It does not ask you to hand the floor to the cloud first. The goal is to make the device in front of you enough to run service.

What makes Slip different

Many POS products start with a feature checklist. Slip starts with the three things that break service first: whether checkout continues offline, whether receipts print when the printer connects, and whether cash balances at close.

That is why Slip is a better fit for people looking for an offline iPad POS than for a broad, generic POS platform.
