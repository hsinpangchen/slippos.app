Cafe POS

A simple iPad POS for cafes that keeps the counter moving.

Slip POS is made for cafes, coffee counters, and small food teams. It does not pull you into a heavy cloud back office first. It starts with the floor: menu setup, fast checkout, receipt printing, and clear cash reconciliation.

Why cafes fit Slip

A cafe POS should preserve service speed. During a morning rush, ordering, checkout, printing, and cash handling need to be obvious to the person at the counter.

Slip is lighter than a full SaaS back office and focuses first on the counter workflow, which makes it practical for small teams that want to start on iPad quickly.

What matters when a cafe gets busy

  • Checkout cannot stop when Wi-Fi is unstable.
  • Menu buttons should be clear without long training.
  • Receipts and tickets should print reliably once the printer connects.

The cafe workflow Slip supports

  • Set up products and categories before opening.
  • Use iPad as the main counter POS.
  • Use iPhone for lighter menu edits or mobile tasks when useful.
  • Review sales and reconcile cash after service.

iPad-first, with iPhone support

For cafes, iPad is the clearest counter POS scenario. Slip also supports iPhone for lighter operational work such as menu adjustments.

That keeps the product easy to understand for people searching for an iPad POS while matching what Slip actually supports.

How it differs from larger cloud POS systems

Many cafe POS systems begin with accounts, dashboards, integrations, and long setup. Slip starts with the counter essentials: offline checkout, printing, and daily records.

For many cafes, a POS tool that works on the floor now is more practical than adopting a full operations platform first.
