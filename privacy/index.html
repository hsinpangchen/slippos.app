Privacy Policy

Slip POS is designed to keep the essential shop workflow simple and local. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Overview

Slip keeps the privacy surface small: collect less, use Apple for purchases, and keep support contact clear.

Information Slip handles

Slip POS does not collect personally identifiable information through the app. To provide core functionality, the app may process subscription and purchase status from Apple, along with basic device information required for the app to run reliably.

How information is used

Information is used only to provide and manage Slip Pro subscription features, restore purchases across devices, and support you when you contact us for help.

Payments and subscriptions

Payment and subscription processing is handled by Apple. Slip does not receive or store your full payment details.

Data sharing

Slip does not sell or share personal data with third parties. Information handled for purchases and subscriptions is processed through Apple services.

Shop data

Operational data created inside Slip is treated as part of your shop workflow. Slip is built around a local-first model, so your core POS data remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@slippos.app.
