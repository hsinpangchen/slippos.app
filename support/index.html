Support

Slip Support

Get help with Slip POS setup, subscriptions, app behavior, or anything that gets in the way of running your shop.

How to reach us

Email is the primary support channel. Include the context of your shop workflow so we can understand the issue clearly.

Contact support

If you need help, please contact us: hello@slippos.app

What to include

Tell us what you were trying to do, what happened, and which device and iOS version you are using. Screenshots are useful when the issue is visual or workflow-related.

Subscriptions and purchases

For App Store purchases, include the Apple ID email only if you are comfortable sharing it. Do not send full payment card details. Apple handles payment processing.

Privacy

For questions about how Slip POS handles privacy and shop data, review the Privacy Policy or contact us directly. View Privacy Policy
